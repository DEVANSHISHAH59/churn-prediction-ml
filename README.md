Customer Churn Prediction Dashboard

This project explores how machine learning can help identify customers who may cancel a service or subscription.

Customer churn is an important problem for many businesses because losing customers directly impacts revenue.

Being able to predict churn helps companies take action earlier.

What the app does

The dashboard allows users to:

• upload a customer dataset
• train a churn prediction model
• estimate churn probability for each customer
• view model explanations
• export a list of high-risk customers

Example use case

A company may want to identify customers who are likely to cancel their subscription.

The model estimates the probability of churn and groups customers into:

Low risk
Medium risk
High risk

Retention teams can then focus on high-risk customers.

Model insights

The app also visualises:

feature importance

churn probability distribution

correlation between variables

This helps users understand why the model makes certain predictions.
